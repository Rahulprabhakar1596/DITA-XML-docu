How to Sum Up Numbers?

Here you will find instructions about using the addition feature of JPS Calculator App

To successfully sum up numbers, you must know what is addition.
  1. Enter the first number
  2. Choose the plus (+) button
  3. Enter the second button
  4. Choose the equal (=) button
The result from the addition is displayed.

You want to add 2 from 3. The equation will look like 2+3=5. You enter 2. Press add. Enter 3. Press equals.

The result 5 will appear on the screen.
