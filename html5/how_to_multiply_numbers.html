How to Multiply Numbers?

Here you will find instructions about using the multiplication feature of JPS Calculator App

To successfully subtract numbers, you must know what is multiplicaton.

  1. Enter the first number
  2. Choose the multiply (*) button
  3. Enter the second number
  4. Choose the equal (=) button

The result from the multiplication is displayed.

You want to multiply 2 and 3. The equation will look like 2*3=6. You enter 2. Press multiply. Enter 3. Press equals.

The result 6 will appear on the screen.
